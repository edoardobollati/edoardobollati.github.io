Working papers

The non-green effects of going green: Local environmental and economic consequences of lithium extraction in Chile

(with Leonardo Peñaloza-Pacheco and Gonzalo Martínez)

In this paper, we analyze the local environmental and economic impacts of lithium extraction in the Atacama Salt Flat (ASF) in Chile. We use groundwater well levels to estimate the impact of the exposure to lithium extraction on water availability. Consequently, we use satellite data to estimate the effects of this exposure on vegetation, local human settlements, and nighttime light density near the ASF.
