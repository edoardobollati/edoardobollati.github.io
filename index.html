Working papers

The non-green effects of “going green”: Local environmental and economic consequences of lithium extraction in Chile

(with Leonardo Peñaloza-Pacheco and Gonzalo Martínez)

In this paper, we analyze the local environmental and economic impacts of lithium extraction in the Atacama Salt Flat (ASF) in Chile. Using satellite data, we compare changes over time in NDVI and human settlements around the ASF and show how they are affected by exposure to lithium extraction.
